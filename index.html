Some places to visit in Manchester

John Rylands Library

Manchester is a thriving city in North West England, known for its industrial past and nicknamed 'Cottonopolis'. However, the city has undergone successful regeneration projects and is now a major hub for culture and commerce. Manchester is often referred to as the capital of the north of England and is sometimes regarded as England's second city.

The Whitworth Art Gallery

The Whitworth is an art gallery in Manchester, England, containing about 55,000 items in its collection. The gallery is located in Whitworth Park and is part of the University of Manchester.
